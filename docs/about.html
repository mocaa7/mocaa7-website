Software Engineer @ Facebook

Seattle, Washington

I attended the University of British Columbia with a Bachelor of Science in Cognitive Systems. Upon graduation, I completed a full-time UX Design program at RED Academy and worked on several projects thereafter. I then completed a Masters of Science in Computer Science at Northeastern University. I was interested in the computer science field because it uses both creative and technical skills, through design and problem solving. What I find sets CS apart from other fields is that you can build programs from almost nothing. It is so rewarding to see your impact almost immediately.

Approach new designs like a child approaches the world: asking WHY. They are free from assumptions that we often take for granted. However, because we are inevitably biased, a clear perspective means evaluating the presence of our biases and how they are affecting our designs, and ultimately, our thoughts.
